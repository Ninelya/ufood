About UFood
What is UFood and how does it work?
UFood is a web based system which provides customers with fast, easy and secure way of food ordering from the restaurants and take aways at their location. No more wasting money and time waiting over the phone to place the order. The service is completely free of charge and prices are equal to prices of the restaurants. Plus you can benefit from our various promotions and special offers provided by the restaurants exclusively at UFood.
